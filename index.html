VIKINGS

Ragnar Lothbrok, a young man who considers himself a descendant of Odin, he is a warrior, farmer and householder. Visionary and dreaming to live several adventures, he believes that the riches dreamed of by his people lie beyond the Baltic Sea. But in order to win them, he needs to convince Earl Haraldson, the leader of his people, to allow him and his warriors to journey towards England.
